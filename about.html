What do the Statistics mean?
Salary: Median Salary within the countries
Total Employed: All currently held positions that have the engineering title (The larger the more job opportunities, but note that these jobs only account for the title for example "Mechanical Engineer")
Job Growth: Expected increase in the total amount of jobs in the next 10 years in the form of a percentage (The higher the better, means there is an increasing demand for the job)
Replacement Rate: Total degrees added into the job market divided by the number of job openings per year (This is a measurement of job saturation, the lower the value the better. A value under 100% means there are more job openings than the supply, causing a shortage. A value higher than 200% is not a good degree as there are 2 people competing for 1 job opening on average.)
