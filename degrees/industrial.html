What is Industrial Engineering?
Industrial engineers are basically the managers of a company. They analyze the entire production process of the company, and integrate all the workers, machines, materials, and energy to make a product of service in the most efficient method possible. They will need to have a strong business and science background to achieve this momentous task.

Health and Safety Engineer
These engineers ensure the environment is safe for everybody. They design systems that minimize the potential for any health or safety hazard, through research and development. They must be proactive in order to anticipate any potential problems in the future, within existing processes.
Manufacturing Engineer
Manufacturing engineers design and manage manufacturing systems, which use machines to assemble products on an assembly line. They choose the best technologies and materials that improve the overall efficiency of the company. If the process already exists, they will monitor existing processes and identify any opportunities to further increase efficiency.
